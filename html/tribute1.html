David Bowie

David Bowie 1947-2016

A singer, songwriter, actor, humanitarian, innovator, fashion icon, and so much more. He changed and evolved as the world around him changed and evolved. There's no easy way to sum up his life. No single article seems to encapsulate all he has done or the influence he has had on the world. The world is a better one for having him in it. Please check out the beautiful piece the New York Times wrote after his death and of course David Bowie's Wikipedia page for a glimpse into the legend.
